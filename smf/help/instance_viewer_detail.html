Instance Viewer

The information displayed on this page corresponds to verbose service information, as gathered by svcs -x. The manual page (if present) in the service information details can be clicked on and viewed, as can the associated logfile(s) and service state explanation.

From this page, the dependency/dependent trees and property groups for the instance can be viewed by clicking the appropriate links at the bottom of the page.
